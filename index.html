Sometimes I am a researcher.

Sometimes I am a poet.

But oftentimes I find myself questioning what I am up to, exactly.

I feel I am in constant preparation for becoming something. The major part of this preparation is learning.

So, for the time being, I identify myself as a learner grappling with the poetics, politics, and pleasures of wrongness, unfinalizibility, and de/reconstruction of the self.
