Zarena Syrgak

I'm a social science researcher at Nazarbayev University and the Frictionless Data for Reproducible Research Fellow. My research interests include, but are not limited to, history and philosophy of science, diversity, equity and inclusion in academic research, open science movements, and postqualitative, new materialist and critical posthumanist methodologies.

Currently I'm an OLS product lead, developing a multilingual open science education space in Central Asian languages. Previously I worked for the Equal Opportunities Social Center for People with Disabilities, American University of Central Asia, Save the Children International, and UN Women.
